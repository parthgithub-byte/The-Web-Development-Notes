Directly using the list <li> tag, also gives out an unordered list or normal bullets. Eg:

  • Nya
  • Ichi
  • Ni
  • San

    • Talking on bullets, the bullets in the unordered list can be given in many available shapes. They're given with their type of shape in the list tags.
    Types of bullets/ unordered list:

    • Zero (Default)
    • One (Circle)
    • Two (Square)
    • Three (None)
    • Four (Disc)
    Thus, by default, disc bullet appears in front of any content in the list tag.
    Similarly, ordered lists can present the order in any form like lowercase or highercase roman, small or capital letters, and in general, normal numbers.
    Eg:
    1. Ek
    2. Don
    3. Tin
    4. Chaar
    5. Paach
    6. Saha
    7. Saat
    8. Aath

    Thus the initials of each type: 1, i, I, a, A are altogether five markers. 1 is the default marker, signifying that an ordered list by default has numerical bullets.
